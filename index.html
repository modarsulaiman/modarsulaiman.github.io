About

I am a PhD student at the University of Tartu, Institute of Computer Science, located in Tartu, Estonia. My research focuses on fairness in machine learning, where I explore methods to ensure equitable and unbiased algorithmic decision-making across diverse populations. Moreover, I am passionate about creating AI solutions that not only perform well technically but also uphold principles of equity and social responsibility.
